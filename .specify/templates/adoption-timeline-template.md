## Kill Criterion

Abandon the adoption if **any two** of the following are true after Phase 2:

1. Gate pass rate remains below 50% despite instruction tuning
2. Majority of team (> 50%) reports increased fatigue
3. Champion is unavailable and no replacement volunteers
4. Time-per-feature increases > 2x with no quality improvement
